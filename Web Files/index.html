Kalki: Harbinger of the End

The Final Battle Between Good and Evil

About the Book

"Kalki: Harbinger of the End" is an epic tale inspired by ancient texts of Vishnu Puran and Kalki Puran. It delves into the prophecy of Lord Kalki's emergence during the dark age of Kali Yuga to restore balance and righteousness in the world.

Book Details

  • Author: Saksham Mahendru
  • Volume: 1 of 3
  • Genre: Mythological Fiction
  • Release Date: Winter 2024

About the Author

Saksham Mahendru is a passionate storyteller with a deep interest in Hindu mythology. "Kalki: Harbinger of the End" is his first novel, crafted with inspiration from the timeless legends of Vishnu Puran and Kalki Puran.
